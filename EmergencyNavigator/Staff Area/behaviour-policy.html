Emergency Navigator Behavior Policy

Emergency Navigator is committed to maintaining a professional, respectful, and inclusive environment for all community members and staff. This Behavior Policy outlines the expectations for staff conduct and the disciplinary actions that may result from violations.

Expected Behaviour

  • Professionalism and Respect: Staff members must treat everyone with respect, courtesy, and professionalism at all times. Discriminatory, harassing, or offensive behavior based on race, gender, sexual orientation, disability, religion, or any other protected characteristic will not be tolerated.
  • Integrity and Honesty: Honesty and integrity are essential. Staff members should always be truthful and transparent in their actions and communications. Plagiarism, dishonesty, or any form of deceit will result in disciplinary action.
  • Confidentiality: Staff members must protect the privacy and confidentiality of sensitive information. Unauthorized disclosure of confidential information will result in immediate disciplinary action.
  • Responsibility and Accountability: Staff members are responsible for their actions and decisions, taking ownership of their work and addressing mistakes promptly.
  • Collaboration and Teamwork: Effective teamwork is crucial. Staff should communicate openly, share information, and support each other.
  • Communication: All communications should be respectful and constructive. Inappropriate language or aggressive behavior will result in disciplinary action.
  • Conflict Resolution: Staff should address conflicts promptly through open dialogue, escalating to higher management if necessary.

Prohibited Behavior

  • Harassment and Bullying: Any form of harassment, bullying, or intimidation, whether verbal, physical, or written, is strictly prohibited. This includes unwanted advances, threats, or behavior that creates a hostile environment.
  • Discrimination: Discrimination based on race, gender, sexual orientation, disability, religion, or any other protected characteristic is strictly prohibited. Equal treatment and opportunities must be provided to all staff members and community participants.
  • Violence and Threats: Any form of violence or threats of violence, including physical altercations or verbal threats, is strictly prohibited and will result in immediate disciplinary action, including termination.
  • Substance Abuse: The use, possession, or distribution of illegal drugs or alcohol while on duty is prohibited. Substance abuse that affects job performance will lead to disciplinary action.
  • Insubordination: Refusing reasonable instructions from supervisors or engaging in defiant behavior will result in disciplinary measures.

Disciplinary Actions

  • Verbal Warning: For minor infractions or first-time offenses, a verbal warning may be issued. The warning will be documented in the staff member's file.
  • Written Warning: For repeated or more serious infractions, a written warning will be issued, outlining the behavior in question and expected improvements.
  • Strike: If behavior persists, a formal strike will be issued, requiring immediate correction.
  • Suspension: For severe or repeated offenses, a temporary suspension may be enforced, depending on the severity of the behavior.
  • Termination: For gross misconduct or continued non-compliance, termination of employment will be considered. Immediate termination may occur in cases of violence, theft, or breaches of confidentiality.

Appeals

Staff members have the right to appeal disciplinary actions. Appeals must be submitted in writing to senior management within seven days of the disciplinary action. The appeal will be reviewed, and a final decision will be communicated to the staff member.

Reporting Misconduct

Staff members are encouraged to report any observed misconduct or violations of this policy. Reports can be made to a supervisor, PR representative, or via email at support@novadesigns.co.uk.

Immediate Disciplinary Action

Our Public Relations Team or Executive Team has the authority to escalate any disciplinary action immediately if necessary.

Conclusion

Adherence to this Behavior Policy is essential for maintaining the integrity and positive atmosphere of Emergency Navigator. All staff members are expected to understand and comply with these guidelines. By fostering a respectful and professional environment, we ensure the success and well-being of our community.
