Phonetic Alphabet

The phonetic alphabet is a standardized set of words used to represent each letter of the alphabet in oral communication. It is used to ensure clarity and avoid misunderstandings, especially in situations where letters might be easily confused, such as over radio or telephone. Each letter is assigned a unique word, making it easier to communicate letters accurately even in noisy or difficult conditions.:
