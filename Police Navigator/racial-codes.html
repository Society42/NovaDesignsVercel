White European

Refers to individuals with a heritage primarily from European countries. This category often includes people with light skin tones and features commonly associated with European ethnic backgrounds.

Dark European

Describes individuals with a European heritage who have a darker skin tone compared to the typical light European complexion. This may include people from southern European countries or those with mixed ancestry.

Black

Represents individuals with African heritage. This category generally refers to people with darker skin tones and features commonly associated with sub-Saharan African ethnic groups.

Asian

Encompasses individuals with ancestry from the Asian continent, including East Asia, Southeast Asia, and South Asia. This category includes a wide range of skin tones and physical features.

Chinese

Refers to individuals with heritage from China. This includes people with typical East Asian features and often lighter skin tones compared to other Asian ethnicities.

Arab

Represents individuals with ancestry from Arab countries in the Middle East and North Africa. This category includes people with a range of skin tones from light to dark and features commonly associated with Arab ethnic groups.

Unknown

This category is used when the ethnic background of an individual cannot be determined or is not specified. It serves as a placeholder for cases where further identification is required.
