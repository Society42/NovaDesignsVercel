Morphine


Dosage: 5-10mg Intravenous (IV), Intraosseous (IO) or intramuscular (IM)


Description: Treats moderate to severe pain by binding to opioid receptors.


Hazzard: Respiratory depression, hypotension, sedation

Paracetamol


Dosage: 500-1000mg Oral (By mouth) or Intravenous (IV)


Description: Mild to moderate pain relief.


Hazzard: Hepatotoxicity (with overdose)

Ketamine


Dosage: 0.1-0.5mg/kg Intravenous (IV) or intramuscular (IM)


Description: Dissociative anesthetic for pain relief and sedation.


Hazzard: Hallucinations, respiratory depression, hypertension

Ibuprofen


Dosage: 400-800mg Oral (By mouth)


Description: Nonsteroidal anti-inflammatory drug (NSAID) for pain relief and fever reduction.


Hazzard: Gastrointestinal bleeding, renal impairment, cardiovascular events

Atropine


Dosage:
0.5mg Intravenous (IV) (Bradycardia)

1mg Intravenous (IV) (Cardiac Arrest)


Description: Raises heart rate by blocking vagal tone.


Hazzard: Tachycardia, dry mouth, blurred vision, urinary retention

Amiodarone


Dosage: 150mg Intravenous (IV)


Description: Treats ventricular arrhythmias, particularly ventricular fibrillation.


Hazzard: Bradycardia, hypotension, QT prolongation, pulmonary toxicity

Adrenaline


Dosage:
0.5-1.0mg Intravenous (IV) or Intraosseous (IO) (Cardiac Arrest, Anaphylaxis)

0.3mg intramuscular (IM) (Severe Asthma)


Description: Raises heart rate, blood pressure, and dilates bronchioles.


Hazzard: Tachycardia, hypertension, arrhythmias, anxiety

TXA (Tranexamic Acid)


Dosage: 1g Intravenous (IV)


Description: Reduces blood loss in hemorrhage and major bleeding.


Hazzard: Thromboembolic events, hypersensitivity reactions

Oxygen (Nasal Cannula)


Dosage: O2 Flow Rate as needed


Description: Provides supplemental oxygen through nasal prongs.


Hazzard: Oxygen toxicity, drying of mucous membranes.

Oxygen (Venturi Mask)


Dosage: O2 Flow Rate as needed


Description: Delivers precise oxygen concentrations.


Hazzard: Same as nasal cannula, risk of CO2 retention.

Oxygen (Non-Rebreather Mask)


Dosage: O2 Flow Rate as needed


Description: Provides highest oxygen concentration for severe hypoxia.


Hazzard: Risk of suffocation if valve malfunctions

Ipratropium Bromide


Dosage: 2.5ml Nebulized


Description: Treats bronchospasm associated with asthma, COPD.


Hazzard: Dry mouth, blurred vision, urinary retention


Adrenaline / Epinephrine


Dosage: 0.5-1.0mg Intravenous (IV) or Intraosseous (IO) (Cardiac Arrest, Anaphylaxis)


Description: Raises heart rate, blood pressure, and dilates bronchioles.


Hazzard: Tachycardia, hypertension, arrhythmias, anxiety

Benadryl


Dosage: 25-50mg Intravenous (IV) or intramuscular (IM)


Description: Antihistamine for allergic reactions.


Hazzard: Sedation, anticholinergic effects, dizziness

Diazepam


Dosage: 2-5mg Intravenous (IV) or intramuscular (IM)


Description: Benzodiazepine for sedation and seizure control.


Hazzard: Respiratory depression, hypotension, sedation

Lorazepam


Dosage: 2mg Intravenous (IV) or intramuscular (IM)


Description: Benzodiazepine for sedation and seizure control.


Hazzard: Respiratory depression, hypotension, sedation

Norepinephrine


Dosage: 0.01-3.0mcg/kg/min Intravenous (IV)


Description: Raises blood pressure in shock states.


Hazzard: Hypertension, tachycardia, arrhythmias

Dopamine


Dosage: 0.5-10.0mcg/kg/min Intravenous (IV)


Description: Raises blood pressure and cardiac output in shock states.


Hazzard: Tachycardia, arrhythmias, extravasation

Aspirin


Dosage: 162-325mg Oral (By mouth)


Description: Inhibits platelet aggregation to prevent clot formation.


Hazzard: Gastrointestinal bleeding, hemorrhagic stroke

Packed Red Blood Cells (PRBC)


Dosage: 1-2 units Intravenous (IV) (as needed)


Description: Provides oxygen-carrying capacity to the blood.


Hazzard: Transfusion reactions, fluid overload, infection risk

Fresh Frozen Plasma (FFP)


Dosage: 10-15mL/kg Intravenous (IV) (as needed)


Description: Replaces clotting factors in patients with coagulopathy.


Hazzard: Transfusion reactions, fluid overload, infection risk

Platelets


Dosage: 1 unit Intravenous (IV) (as needed)


Description: Treats thrombocytopenia or platelet dysfunction.


Hazzard: Transfusion reactions, fluid overload, infection risk

Intravenous Fluids


Dosage: As needed Intravenous (IV)


Description: Normal Saline


Hazzard: Provides fluid resuscitation and electrolyte balance
