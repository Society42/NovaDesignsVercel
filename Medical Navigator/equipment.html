Medical Equipment

Administration Folder

Features: Contains necessary paperwork, patient care records, and documentation forms.

Uses: Helps in maintaining accurate patient records and administrative tasks.

Hazards/Limitations: Paperwork can be time-consuming and needs to be updated regularly to ensure accuracy.

Ballistic Protection Vests & Ballistic Helmets (S, M & L)

Features: Protective vests or helmets are in various sizes designed to safeguard personnel from ballistic threats.

Uses: Ensures safety of ambulance personnel in hazardous situations involving firearms.

Hazards/Limitations: Can be bulky and restrict movement. Not useful in all situations.

Body Bags

Features: Durable, sealable bags for the respectful transportation of deceased individuals.

Uses: Used for the secure and dignified handling of deceased patients.

Hazards/Limitations: Requires careful handling to maintain dignity and prevent contamination.

Braun Alcohol Hand Sanitiser

Features: Alcohol-based sanitiser for hand hygiene.

Uses: Reduces the risk of infection by killing germs on hands.

Hazards/Limitations: May cause skin irritation with frequent use. Ineffective against certain types of germs.

Carry Sheets

Features: Sturdy sheets designed to carry patients safely.

Uses: Assists in the safe transport of patients, particularly in difficult-to-reach areas.

Hazards/Limitations: Requires multiple personnel to operate safely. Incorrect use can lead to injury.

Cervical Collar

Features: Rigid or semi-rigid collars for neck immobilization.

Uses: Prevents movement of the cervical spine in trauma patients.

Hazards/Limitations: Improper application can cause discomfort or further injury. Prolonged use can lead to pressure sores.

Clinical Waste Bin

Features: Secured container for the disposal of medical waste.

Uses: Ensures safe and hygienic disposal of contaminated materials.

Hazards/Limitations: Must be emptied regularly to prevent overflow and contamination risks.

Command Pack (Major Incident Kit)

Features: Comprehensive kit for managing major incidents, including command and control tools.

Uses: Assists in the coordination and management of large-scale emergencies.

Hazards/Limitations: Requires training for effective use. Can be complex to manage in high-stress situations.

Collision Investigation Equipment

Features: Tools and devices used to investigate and document accident scenes.

Uses: Helps in the thorough investigation of vehicle collisions to understand causes and effects.

Hazards/Limitations: Requires training to use effectively. Can be time-consuming to deploy.

Disposable Latex Gloves (S, M, L, XL)

Features: Single-use gloves in various sizes to protect hands during patient care.

Uses: Prevents cross-contamination and protects against infectious agents.

Hazards/Limitations: Can cause allergic reactions in some individuals. Must be disposed of after each use.

Dressings Pack

Features: A variety of dressings for wound care, including bandages, gauze, and adhesive strips.

Uses: Used to cover and protect wounds, control bleeding, and prevent infection.

Hazards/Limitations: Incorrect application can lead to inadequate wound care. Requires regular restocking.
