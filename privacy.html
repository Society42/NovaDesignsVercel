Welcome to Emergency Navigator, a website operated by Nova Designs. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our website.

1. Information We Collect

We do not store any personal information such as ages you may enter upon accessing our site. Any data you provide is stored locally on your own device using local storage, not on our database. This means you have full control over your data and can remove it at any time.

2. Local Storage

Local storage allows us to store data locally on your device to enhance your user experience. This data is not transmitted to our servers and can be cleared by you at any time through your browser settings.

3. Cookies

We use cookies to gain analytics and help improve our site. Cookies are small text files placed on your device to collect standard internet log information and visitor behavior information. This information is used to track visitor use of the website and to compile statistical reports on website activity.

Types of Cookies We Use

  • Analytics Cookies: These cookies collect information about how visitors use our website, such as which pages are visited most often. This data helps us improve the functionality and performance of our site.
  • Functional Cookies: These cookies allow our website to remember choices you make (such as your username, language, or the region you are in) and provide enhanced, more personal features.

4. Managing Cookies

You can set your browser not to accept cookies. If you choose to disable cookies, some features of our website may not function properly.

5. Data Security

We are committed to ensuring that your information is secure. To prevent unauthorized access or disclosure, we have put in place suitable physical, electronic, and managerial procedures to safeguard and secure the information we collect online.
