Privacy Policy

At Nova Designs, we are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect and use your personal information.

Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information:

  • Personal identification information (e.g., name, email address) provided voluntarily by users.
  • Non-personal identification information such as browser type, language preference, and referring site.
  • Cookies and similar technologies to enhance user experience and analyze site usage.

Use of Information

We use collected information for the following purposes:

  • To personalize user experience and improve our services.
  • To send periodic emails regarding updates, promotions, or other relevant information (users may opt-out at any time).
  • To respond to inquiries and provide customer support.

Data Security

We implement security measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction.

Sharing Your Information

We do not sell, trade, or rent users' personal identification information to others. We may share generic aggregated demographic information not linked to any personal identification information with trusted partners for the purposes outlined above.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We encourage you to review this page for any changes. You acknowledge and agree that it is your responsibility to review this Privacy Policy periodically and become aware of modifications.
