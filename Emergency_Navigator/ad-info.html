Our Advertising Banners

Our advertising banners showcase all our partners. You can see them around the site, but we aim to keep them unobtrusive and easy to close. Click on the "Learn More" button in an ad banner to be taken directly to the partner's server. If no specific ad is available, you will be directed to this page for more information.

Ads will be showcased in the advertisement banners and on our collaboration page. To learn more about our advertising partners, join our Discord server. Additionally, make sure to check out our Terms of Service and Privacy Policy.

Why We Use Advertisements

Advertisements help us keep our services free and support the development and maintenance of the platform. We carefully select our advertising partners to ensure they align with our values and the interests of our community.

How to Become an Advertising Partner

Interested in becoming a partner? Reach out to us via our Discord server to get in touch with our team. We look forward to collaborating with you.
