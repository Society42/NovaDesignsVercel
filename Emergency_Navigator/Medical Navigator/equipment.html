Emergency Services Equipment List

Administration Folder

  • Features: Contains necessary paperwork, patient care records, and documentation forms.
  • Uses: Helps in maintaining accurate patient records and administrative tasks.
  • Hazards/Limitations: Paperwork can be time-consuming and needs to be updated regularly.

Ballistic Protection Vests & Helmets

  • Features: Protective vests and helmets designed to safeguard personnel from ballistic threats.
  • Uses: Ensures safety of personnel in hazardous situations involving firearms.
  • Hazards/Limitations: Can be bulky and restrict movement. Not useful in all situations.

Body Bags

  • Features: Durable, sealable bags for respectful transportation of deceased individuals.
  • Uses: Used for secure and dignified handling of deceased patients.
  • Hazards/Limitations: Requires careful handling to prevent contamination.
